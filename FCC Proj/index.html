Care and Maintenance

Statues with designs that may retain or collect water should be covered or stored. They are also susceptible to damage by effects of ice and wind. We strongly suggest that all portable statues be taken indoors in extremely inclement weather and freezing temperatures.

Shipping Services

Shipping statues is an important service. Our goal is to get your statue to you without damage; therefore, extra attention must be paid to packaging and shipping of statuary not ordinarily found in shipping of other goods.
